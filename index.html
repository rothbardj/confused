About

Science in service of
the people who need it most

For decades, Jonathan Rothbard has worked at the highest levels of medical science — from doctoral research at Columbia University to cancer drug development at Stanford and the Imperial Cancer Research Fund. His career has been devoted to a single pursuit: understanding, at the molecular level, how disease works and how to stop it.

Today, he brings that same depth of knowledge to individuals and families navigating complex, high-stakes medical decisions. When a diagnosis arrives, most people face an overwhelming flood of clinical language, treatment options, and competing advice. Jonathan sits with them — as a scientist, as a translator, as a guide — and helps them truly understand what they are facing and what their real options are.

This is not general health advocacy. This is rare scientific expertise, brought to bear on your specific situation, at the moment it matters most.
